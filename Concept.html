When I started to work on the webpage for my projects, I had no clue how it would like, just the projects that I wanted to involve. My first idea was a cork board design. The text and the pictures would have been on sticky notes placed on the board.

While searching webpages for more inspiration, I got an idea that became the final design. The design is based on the classic blueprints, seen among others in the TV show Mythbusters. I tried to stick to the design during the coding process. I managed to find this simple, but cool wallpaper matching my ideas and I used Permanent Marker font type by Google Fonts.
