In 2019 I participated in the World Robot Olympiad with two friends. Our team was called New Crew.

WRO is a global Lego robot competition, where elementary schoolers and high schoolers can compete in various categories, such as: robot football, open category (the competitors are given a kind of project task, which they need to present), ARC(Advanced Robotics Challenge for university students) and regular category(this category is divided into 4 age groups: WeDo for nursery schoolers, Elementary for elementary schoolers, Junior for middle schoolers and Senior for high schoolers).

New Crew entered in Regular Senior. We were given a challenge in advance and had to design a robot construction and write a code used by the robot while driving autonomously on the field given by the organisers. We managed to qualify to the national final from the regional, but unfortunately took
the 15 th place out of 20, and we did not go on to participate in the global final, which was organised in Győr.
